Frequently Asked Questions

Who are these apps intended for?
These applications are designed for qualified healthcare professionals including doctors, pharmacists, and specialist clinicians.

Do the apps provide medical advice?
No. These apps are for educational and clinical reference purposes only and do not replace professional clinical judgment.

Do the apps collect personal data?
No. These applications do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Do the apps require internet connection?
No. Core functionality works fully offline.

Medical Disclaimer

These applications are intended for educational and reference use only. They do not provide medical advice and must not be used as a substitute for professional clinical judgment, local protocols, or national guidelines. Always verify information using official sources such as BNF or SPC.
