Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this app free?
Please refer to the App Store listing for current pricing.

Does the app work offline?
Yes. The app is fully functional without internet access.

Does the app store patient data?
No. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any patient data.

Can I rely on this app for clinical decisions?
The app supports clinical decision-making but does not replace clinical judgement or local guidelines.

Which guidelines is the app based on?
The app is based on JBDS 2023 guidelines for DKA management.
